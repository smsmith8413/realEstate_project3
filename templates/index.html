Real Estate Insights: Home Sales and Price Drivers
in Collin County, Texas

Overview

Welcome to our site! If you are a Realtor, prospective home buyer or prospective home seller, let us help you gain insight into a fair and realistic price on a home in Collin County, TX. We have collected 17 years of home sales from the Multiple Listing Service (MLS) for Collin county to help you better understand long-term marketplace trends to current market trends that will impact price and help you think about future. If you would like to learn more about a homes potential price feel free to input the homes attributes and features for these five Collin County Cities (Frisco, Fairview, Prosper, Plano, McKinney) into our Price Prediction tool.

Conclusion

In conclusion, we found via this project helped us understand key factors in real estate price drivers through the machine learning and via Tableau visualizations. Through the Price Prediction Tool we are able to determine a an estimated price to sell or purchase price. Key factors found via Machine Learning and utilized in the Price Predictor tool.

  • Original List Price (OLP)
  • Days on Market (DOM)
  • Postal Code
  • Square Feet
  • Year Built
  • Total Beds
  • Total Baths
  • Parking Spaces Garage
  • Pool

    • We looked at other features and delved deeper visually into these factors via Tableau that showed the team other interesting that a Seller or Buyer should think about.

    Occupancy plays a factor as well as Owner Occupied homes generally sold for higher prices and more per square foot as well than Vacant and Tenant Occupied.

    With DOM we found that for the five cities generally ~75% of the homes sold within 60 days of listing with a 1-2% variance from original list price for homes sold in 30 days and a 3-5% variance from homes sold in 31-60 days. There was a trend where the ratio between Original List Price (OLP) and Close Price (CP) grew larger as the DOM was longer with homes on the market greater 210 had saw a 10-15% difference in OLP vs CP.

    We also looked at the inputs of attributes and features to have a more granular look into the data and how pricing behaves as attributes and features are changed. These attribute and feature changes can help sellers and buyers better understand a market and the impacts of various counts pertaining to Bedroom, Bathroom, Living Areas, Dining Areas, Stories, Garages, etc.

    Lastly, we found this project was a great way to learn about real estate price factors and to educate Sellers and Buyers in an impactful way using Machine Learning and Data Visualization.
